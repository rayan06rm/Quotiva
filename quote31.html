"You miss 100% of the shots you don’t take."

- Wayne Gretzky -

About the Author

Wayne Gretzky (born 1961) is a former Canadian ice hockey player, widely regarded as one of the greatest athletes in the history of the sport. Known as "The Great One," he holds numerous records in the NHL and is celebrated for his vision, skill, and leadership on and off the ice.

Understanding the Quote

Why did he write this?
Gretzky emphasized the importance of taking risks and seizing opportunities. In hockey, as in life, failing to act guarantees failure, while taking a chance opens the door to success.

When?
While the exact date of this quote is uncertain, it has been widely attributed to Gretzky throughout his career and remains a fundamental principle in sports, business, and personal growth.

What does it mean?
This quote underscores the idea that inaction leads to guaranteed failure. If you don’t try, you automatically fail. But by taking chances—whether in sports, career, or life—you increase your chances of success. Even if you fail, you gain experience that helps you improve.

How This Quote Can Inspire You

If you're hesitant to pursue a goal, remember that inaction guarantees failure. Whether it’s applying for a job, starting a business, or taking on a challenge, success only comes when you take the shot. Even if you miss, you learn and grow, bringing you closer to future success.
